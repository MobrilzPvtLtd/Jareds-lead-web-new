Common Questions

Mailing List & Data FAQs

Everything you need to know about mailing lists, pricing, and data quality.

What is a mailing list?
A mailing list is a collection of contact data such as names, addresses, or emails used for marketing campaigns like direct mail, email outreach, and telemarketing.
How much does a mailing list cost?
Pricing varies by list type, targeting criteria, and volume. Costs typically range from a few cents to several dollars per record depending on quality and specificity.
What types of mailing lists are available?
We offer business mailing lists, consumer mailing lists, email lists, telemarketing lists, direct mail services, and custom-built targeted data.
How do I buy a targeted email list?
You can request a custom list by defining your audience criteria such as industry, job title, location, or demographics. Our team builds and verifies your list before delivery.
What is the difference between a mailing list and an email list?
A mailing list contains physical postal addresses for direct mail campaigns, while an email list contains verified email addresses for digital marketing outreach.
How often is your data updated?
Our data is continuously updated through multiple source feeds, NCOA processing, and regular hygiene cycles to ensure maximum accuracy and deliverability.
Do you offer data hygiene and list cleaning services?
Yes. Our Data Services include CASS certification, NCOA processing, deduplication, email verification, and data appending.
Can I get a sample list before purchasing?
Yes — contact us to request a sample. We want you to be confident in the data quality before you commit.
